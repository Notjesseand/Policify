What are the Terms and Conditions?

The terms and conditions agreement is a website page or document that clarifies the rights, responsibilities, terms, conditions, and usages of anyone visisting the site. Essentially, it is a simple way to create a contract between the site owner and site users. Terms and conditions usuaally feature an explanation of any key phrases used in terms and conditions agreement, and an outline of the restrictions on the legal liability of the website owner incase of any damages incurred during use of the website. It will also include the website's policy for any legal action that could be taken against any user that break the terms and provide users with legal advice as to the rights of the website owner to do so.
